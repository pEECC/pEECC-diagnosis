1. Resources

Based on the EECC Rapid Health Facility Assessment Tool PART 1 June 2025: Health facility EECC Resource Availability & Readiness.

Method from the EECC tool

Facility-level Availability: 10 minutes of questions for the facility in-charge or other knowledgeable person in the facility. Ask the questions, circling “Y” for Yes or “N” for No. Add up points and divide by 25, then multiply by 100.

Ward/Bedside-level Readiness: 35 items to observe in each ward/unit of the facility together with the ward in-charge. Repeat assessment for each ward/unit. Calculate the facility readiness score by adding up each ward/unit score and dividing by number of wards/units assessed.
